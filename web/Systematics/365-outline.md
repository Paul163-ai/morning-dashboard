# A Year in Systematic Theology — 365-Day Outline

Structured loosely after Wayne Grudem's *Systematic Theology*, arranged as 52 weeks
(7 days each) + 1 capstone day. Each week stays on one doctrine, moving from
definition → biblical basis → implications → application, so a reader gets a full
"mini-course" on that topic across the week while each day still stands alone.

Format per day: **Verse · Explanation · Prayer · Reflection question**

---

### Notes on execution
- Each numbered topic above spans roughly 2–4 devotional days as we flesh the year out,
  so the 114 topics stretch naturally to fill 364 days, with each day narrowing to one
  facet, verse, and application rather than repeating the whole topic.
- I'll draft in batches (e.g. one week / ~7 days at a time, or by section) so you can
  sanity-check tone and theology as we go, rather than generating all 365 at once.
